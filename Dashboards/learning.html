🇰🇪 Kenya Data Protection Act (2019)

Right to be Informed 📢

Know exactly what is tracked before you click "Accept".
Section 26(a): Websites must clearly state what data they collect and why.

Right to Access 📂

See the digital profile they have built on you.
Section 26(b): You can demand a copy of all personal data a company holds.

Right to Erasure 🗑️

"Right to be Forgotten" - delete your history.
Section 40(1)(b): Demand deletion if data is no longer needed or you withdraw consent.

Right to Object ✋

Stop them from using your data for ads.
Section 26(c): You can opt-out of processing for direct marketing at any time.

Correction ✏️

Fix false information about you.
Section 26(d): If their data (like credit info or location) is wrong, they MUST fix it.

Portability 📦

Move your data to another service.
Section 38: You have the right to receive your data in a standard format (like CSV).

🇪🇺 GDPR Standards

Active Consent ✅

Silence is not consent. No pre-ticked boxes.
The Standard: You must actively click "Agree" for non-essential cookies.

No Profiling 🤖

Opt-out of automated decision making.
The Standard: Algorithms shouldn't decide your credit score or job eligibility without human review.

2. Cookies Explained: The Good, The Bad & The Ugly

Not all cookies are the same. Here is the breakdown of what you are actually accepting.

LOW RISK

Functional / Session

Keeps you logged in and remembers your shopping cart. Deleted when you close the browser.

✅ Advice: Always Accept.
MED RISK

Analytics

Tracks which pages you visit and how long you stay (e.g., Google Analytics).

⚖ Advice: Optional. Helps the site owner, but tracks you.
HIGH RISK

Marketing / 3rd Party

Follows you across DIFFERENT websites to build a profile and sell ads.

🛑 Advice: Always Reject.

3. Privacy Toolkit: Actionable Tips

Don't just click "Accept All"

Most banners have a hidden "Manage Settings" button.

The 5-Second Rule: Spend 5 seconds to find "Manage Options".
✅ Enable Strictly Necessary.
❌ Disable Performance & Marketing categories.

The "Incognito" Advantage

Ideal for one-off searches or reading news articles on sites with paywalls/cookie-walls.

Why it works: When you close an Incognito/Private window, the browser immediately destroys all cookies collected in that session. No long-term tracking.

Digital Hygiene Routine

Cookies accumulate like dust. Clean them out.

Monthly Task: Go to Browser Settings > Privacy > Clear Browsing Data.
Select "Cookies and Site Data" and wipe it. This resets your digital footprint.
