1. Know Your Rights

Click any card to reveal details about how this law protects you.

🇪🇺 GDPR: Right to Access 📂

You can ask any company for a copy of ALL data they hold on you.
How to use: Send a "Subject Access Request" (SAR) email. They must respond within 30 days for free.

🇪🇺 GDPR: Right to Erasure 🗑️

Also known as the "Right to be Forgotten". Ask them to delete you.
When it works: If the data is no longer needed or you withdraw consent.

🇪🇺 GDPR: Right to Object ✋

Stop them from using your data for marketing/sales.
Power Move: You can object to "Direct Marketing" at any time, and they MUST stop immediately.

🇰🇪 DPA: Informed Consent 📝

You must be told CLEARLY what data is taken and why.
Red Flag: If a privacy policy is hidden or too complex, it violates the Kenya DPA.

🇰🇪 DPA: Right to Correct ✏️

False data about you? You can demand it be fixed.
Why it matters: Wrong credit scores or medical info can harm you real-world.

🤖 Automated Decisions ⚙️

Right not to be judged solely by AI/Algorithms.
Example: If a bank loan is denied by a computer, you can demand a human review it.

2. Cookies Explained: The Good, The Bad & The Ugly

Not all cookies are the same. Here is the breakdown of what you are actually accepting.

LOW RISK

Functional / Session

Keeps you logged in and remembers your shopping cart. Deleted when you close the browser.

✅ Advice: Always Accept.
MED RISK

Analytics

Tracks which pages you visit and how long you stay (e.g., Google Analytics).

⚖️ Advice: Optional. Helps the site owner, but tracks you.
HIGH RISK

Marketing / 3rd Party

Follows you across DIFFERENT websites to build a profile and sell ads.

🛑 Advice: Always Reject.

3. Real Life Scenarios

You are buying sneakers online.

The banner asks to "Accept All". If you reject all, your cart might empty itself.

Best Action: Choose "Customize" or "Manage Preferences".
✅ Accept Functional (for the cart).
❌ Reject Marketing (so the shoes don't follow you as ads).

A news site blocks you (Cookie Wall).

"We value your privacy, but you must accept cookies to read this article."

Best Action: Use Incognito Mode to read it. When you close the window, the cookies are destroyed immediately.

"Log in with Facebook/Google"

It saves time creating a new password, but...

Best Action: Avoid if possible. This links your shopping habits directly to your social media profile, allowing highly targeted data profiling.
